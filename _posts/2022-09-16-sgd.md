# Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD) & Variants

In deep learning, it is common to minimize a **loss function**. This poses an **optimization** problem. A very common **optimizing algorithm** is the **stochastic gradient descent (sgd)**. There are a lot of materials covering it, therefore we will not go deeper into it. We will address the basic idea of it and its variants:

* SGD
* SGD + Momentum
* Nesterov Momentum
* Adagrad
* RMSProp
* Adam 

### SGD
